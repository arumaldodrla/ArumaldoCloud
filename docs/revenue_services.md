# Revenue Services and Upsell Opportunities: Arumaldo Cloud

## 1. Introduction

This document outlines the various revenue-generating services and upsell opportunities for the Arumaldo Cloud platform. The goal is to provide a comprehensive suite of services that enhance security, performance, and compliance, while creating additional revenue streams. All services are designed to be provisioned and managed through a fully automated, self-service model.

## 2. SSL Certificate Services

Arumaldo Cloud will act as an SSL reseller, offering a range of certificates to meet different customer needs. This is a core revenue-generating service that complements the platform's security-first approach.

### 2.1 SSL Reseller Partnership

- **Recommended Partner:** Sectigo or GoGetSSL for a balance of features and cost-effectiveness.
- **Integration:** API-based integration for automated certificate issuance, validation, and renewal.
